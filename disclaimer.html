CESW Staff Hub Disclaimer

Last updated:

1) Staff Use Only

The CESW Staff Hub is provided for authorised staff use. Access is restricted to current staff members and approved administrators.

2) Appropriate Use

You must use the Hub in line with school policies and applicable laws. Do not post or upload content that is confidential, unlawful, offensive, or infringes third-party rights.

3) Monitoring & Logging

For security and support, the system may log sign-ins, IP addresses, and activity metadata (e.g., last sign-in time). Logs are used to protect users and the platform.

4) Privacy

We collect only what’s necessary to operate the Hub (e.g., name, email, role). Data is handled in accordance with school privacy obligations. Contact ICT if you have privacy questions.

5) Accuracy & Availability

While we aim for accuracy and uptime, content may change and services may be interrupted. The Hub is provided “as is” without warranties.

6) Third-Party Links

The Hub may link to external sites. We’re not responsible for the content or policies of third-party services.

7) Security

Keep your credentials secure. Report suspicious activity to ICT immediately. We may lock accounts after repeated failed sign-in attempts.

8) Agreement

By signing in, you acknowledge you’ve read and agree to this Disclaimer.
